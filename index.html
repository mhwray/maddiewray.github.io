What I Do

As an artist I both am apart of and curate performing art pieces. With fellow artist my work is designed to have human interaction. To inspire, impact, question and drive the arts community. I embody dance to tell stories and present ideas in a new light to all walks of life.

Who I Am

A passionate dancer that has been with and apart of this artform for over a decade. An artist that is constantly growing with and from others as well as finding what my path is. Constantly using my brain to think outside of the box and ustilizing the tools that I have learned to reimagine dance live and through film. Dance lives within and through me. This form continues to teach me new things every day and I constantly desire to share what I know and love with others.
